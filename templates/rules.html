Platform Overview

How Interview Analyzer Works

Everything you need to know about the technology, the process, and what your results actually mean.

The Problem We Solve

Most interview preparation misses the most important part

Candidates spend hours rehearsing answers — but they have no idea how they actually come across. Facial expressions, vocal tone, and word choice all shape the impression an interviewer forms, often without either party realising it.

Interview Analyzer makes the invisible visible. Record yourself, submit your response, and receive an objective AI-powered breakdown of how you are perceived — not just what you say, but how you say it.

😰

Before Interview Analyzer

You rehearse answers alone. No idea if you look nervous. No idea if your voice sounds tense. No feedback until the rejection email.

🤖

After Interview Analyzer

You know exactly which emotions showed on your face, what your voice revealed, and how you compare — before the real interview.

Step by Step

What Happens When You Submit

1

Choose Your Mode

Select Video or Audio depending on which dimension of your performance you want to train. Each uses a completely different AI model targeting a different signal.

2

Record or Upload Your Response

Answer a real interview question live through your browser, or upload a file you already have. Answer naturally — as you would in a real interview.

3

AI Analysis Runs Automatically

The moment you submit, our deep learning pipeline processes your input. For video, every frame is passed through a facial emotion classifier. For audio, acoustic features are extracted and classified. No human reviews your submission.

4

Get Your Results Dashboard

Your results page shows a readiness score, emotion breakdown, trend chart, personalised feedback, and a benchmark against other candidates. Every insight is generated specifically from your submission.

Analysis Modules

Two AI Models, Two Dimensions

🎥

Video Analysis

Computer vision tracks your face frame by frame. A convolutional neural network classifies each frame into one of seven emotional states, producing an overall breakdown and a timeline of emotional shifts.

OpenCV dlib CNN D3.js charts

Detects

Angry · Disgust · Fear · Happy · Sad · Surprise · Neutral

🎤

Audio Analysis

Speech emotion recognition extracts acoustic features — MFCC, chroma, spectral contrast — from your voice. A deep learning model classifies the emotional content independently of the words you speak.

Librosa MFCC Deep Learning Benchmarking

Detects

Angry · Disgust · Fear · Happy · Sad · Surprise · Neutral

Reading Your Results

What Each Part of Your Dashboard Means

🎯

Readiness Score

A single 0–100 score aggregating your emotional profile. Colour-coded based on how interview-ready your expressions appeared.

📊

Emotion Breakdown

What percentage of your recording was dominated by each emotion. Identifies which signals are strongest and whether they help or hurt.

📈

Timeline Chart

A D3.js chart showing emotion intensity across your recording. Pinpoints exactly when emotional shifts occurred.

👥

Benchmark

Your distribution vs the average of all previous candidates. Context that makes your score meaningful.

💡

Personalised Feedback

Generated specifically from your numbers — not a generic tips list. Addresses exactly what your results show.

⬇️

Download Report

Save your results as a PDF. Track how your scores improve with repeated practice sessions.
